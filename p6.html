#6 Data Portrait

Description

The data I track is my iPhone screen time. I'm trying to cut down the time I use my cellphone so I think it's a great opportunity to visualize this everyday habit.

Instruction

TI tracked 6 days of screen time. Each bar represents one day of data and the x(length) equals to time of day, and y(height) equals how long do I use the phone.

Design Process

TBT... (numbered list, sketches/images)

Overall Reflection

In the first three days of the tracking, I found that the time I used the phone was very fixed. The peak of data usually happens at a specific time, that is, after waking up, noon, and before sleep. Through the data, I learned that my daily habits are almost very fixed and regular. Although there are some minor changes, there are still traces to follow.

So on the fourth day, the fourth day, in order to experiment with the changes in data, I deleted the social media apps I use most often, which is, Facebook and Instagram. Through data visualization, you can see a significant drop in the data in the fourth column of the chart. Through direct vision, I can see my changes more clearly. This is a very useful strategy for me to further maintain my habits.
